The Power of Compound Interest

In sports trading and bankroll management, compounding is your most powerful tool. It is the process where your profits earn more profits. Instead of withdrawing your daily gains, you add them back to your stake to grow your capital exponentially.

How to use this simulator:

  1. Initial Bankroll: Your starting capital for betting or investing.
  2. Daily/Monthly Rate: The average percentage you aim to win (e.g., a 2% profit per day).
  3. Time Period: How long you plan to reinvest (days or months).

Pro Tip: An arbitrage bettor who makes 1% profit daily and reinvests it can double their bankroll in about 70 days. Consistency beats luck.
