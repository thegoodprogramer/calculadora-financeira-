What is Arbitrage Betting?

Arbitrage betting occurs when bookmakers have different odds for the same event. By betting on all possible outcomes, you can guarantee a profit regardless of the result.

How to use:

  1. Enter the odds for both outcomes.
  2. Enter your total stake.
  3. The tool calculates exactly how much to bet on each side.
